Property Overview

Siddhraj Zavod is a premier business park featuring high-end corporate offices and innovative workspaces designed for comfort and productivity. Strategically located on the thriving S G Highway, it boasts a modern façade with energy-efficient sun breakers, an inviting entrance lobby, and ample common areas. The facility includes three high-quality automatic elevators, column-free large showrooms, and flexible workspace designs that allow for customization. Each unit features vitrified tile flooring and attached toilets, ensuring a functional and luxurious working environment.
