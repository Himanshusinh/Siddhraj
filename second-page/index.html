Siddhraj Z2

Modern Urban Plaza with Premium Office and Retail Spaces

Property Overview

The property boasts a prime location with a 100-foot wide plaza, featuring parking, seating areas, water bodies, and recreational spaces. It offers easy access through ample lifts and a link road connecting two streets via the plaza. The buildings are connected by an urban plaza and a 30-foot wide walkway on the second floor. There are two levels of basement parking for owners, staff, and visitors. A triple-height entrance foyer with Italian marble flooring leads to the offices. The property has 22 high-speed elevators, sitting decks on each floor, and a double-height recreational area on the third floor. The offices are airy, well-ventilated, and surround open courtyards. A landscaped sky bridge connects the buildings on the 5th floor, and there are provisions for AC pipelines and a dedicated service area for AC units
