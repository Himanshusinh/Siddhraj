Property Overview

Siddhraj Zori, located on a 12-meter service road along S.G. Highway, features strategically positioned showrooms for optimal commercial use while fostering a serene environment for office spaces. The development incorporates advanced systems tailored to meet the diverse needs of both office and retail users. Key specifications include efficient column arrangements for versatile designs, a spacious lift lobby, CCTV surveillance in common areas, ample visitor parking, high-speed elevators with an eight-passenger capacity, and an advanced building management system to ensure maximum utilization of office spaces
